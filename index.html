About

I am an Assistant Professor in the department of Smart Security @ Gachon University. Prior to joining GCU, I was a Researcher @ Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI). I received the BS and MS degrees in electronics engineering, and the PhD degree in electronic and electrical engineering from Kyungpook National University (KNU), South Korea, in 2015, 2017, and 2021, respectively.

My research interests include trustworthy AI, AI/ML security, authentication, blockchain, network security, decentralized identifier, internet of things, privacy-preserving techniques, post-quantum cryptography, VA/FANET, and information security.

My lab is always open to highly motivational, enthusiastic and proactive graduate/ undergraduate students. I am also looking for postdocs to work with our researches.
If you are interested in joining our member, please contact me with your CV or information.
